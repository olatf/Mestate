Tenant Services

Whether you are looking for cozy apartment in the city or a spacious family home in the suburbs, we offer a diverse range of rental properties to suit your lifestyle and budget. Our team is here to guide you through the entire rental process, from search to move-in. We also provide legal coverage to avoid any frustration before, during and after tenancy period.

Property Management

For property owners, we provide comprehensive management services, including tenant screening, lease agreements, maintenance and rent collection. We also provide legal coverage for landlords to avoid any frustration from some tenants. Our goal is to maximize your rental income while minimizing your workload.

Lease

We have office complex, rental apartments and lands, event centres for lease. Residential leases are generally shorter (1 year), while commercial leases can be much longer (3-10 years). Usually, negotiation is always allowed with any lease.
