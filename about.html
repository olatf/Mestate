Overview

Welcome to Mestate (a division of Dynamest Ltd), where passion meets innovation. Our journey began in 2011 with a simple idea of general house/office cleaning and laundry delivered to make positive impact on our immediate world through dynamic service delivery. Today, we have undergone tremendous metamorphorsis. We are very experienced in house-keeping and maintenance, and have, therefore, been concerned about exorbitant house rent that tenants pay every year.

Overview

Our technology has place us at the forefront in the real estate industry because it has brought solution to reduce the exorbitant house rent experienced in Nigeria by bringing together tenants and landlords at very little cost – this, on both sides. No more will there be a queue of agents on just one tenant, making his house rent soar. Another important benefit is that of our central legal system that will cover those on Mestate platform so that any legal issue will not be a problem for either the tenant or the landlord. All problem solved.
