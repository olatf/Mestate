FAQ

....... Our well detailed FAQ can help you navigate our site fully.......

How do I apply for a rental property?

To apply for a rental property, visit our website and fill out the online application form. Pay for inspection fee and our Representative will immediately get in touch with you to tidy up the rest of the documentations

What documents do I need to provide with my application?

You will need to provide a valid government-issued ID, proof of income (bank statement for the past six {6} months), work address and rental history references.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of Two thousand naira Only (NGN2,000.00). This fee covers the cost of processing your application, including a credit and background check.

How long is the lease term?

Our standard lease term is 12 months. However, we may offer shorter or longer lease terms depending on the property and your needs.

How is the rent payment processed?

Rent can be paid online through our tenant portal or direct transfer to Dynamest Ltd. We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and checks.

When is rent due?

Rent is due on the first of each month. Late fees may apply if rent is not received by the fifth of the month.

Can I make modifications to the rental property?

Any modifications to the property must be approved by our office in writing. Unauthorized modifications may result in penalties.

Can I break my lease early?

Breaking a lease early may result in penalties. Please contact our office to discuss your specific situation and the possible fees involved./p>

How do I request maintenance or repairs?

Maintenance requests can be submitted through our online tenant portal or by calling our office during business hours. For emergency repairs, please contact our 24/7 emergency hotline.

Who is responsible for yard maintenance?

Yard maintenance responsibilities vary by property. Please refer to your lease agreement for specific details regarding your rental property.

What should I do if I have a pest problem?

Please report any pest issues to our office immediately. We will arrange for pest control services to address the problem.

What is the move-in process?

After your application is approved and the lease is signed, we will schedule a move-in date. On this date, you will receive the keys and a move-in checklist to document the condition of the property.

What is the move-out process?

Prior to moving out, you will need to provide written notice as specified in your lease. On your move-out date, you should return the keys and ensure the property is clean and in good condition, exactly you met it. A final inspection will be conducted to assess any damages.

Will I get my security deposit back?

Your security deposit will be refunded within 3 days after you move out, provided there are no damages or unpaid rent. Deductions may be made for any necessary repairs or cleaning.
